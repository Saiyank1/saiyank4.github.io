How to become a Pilot

An aircraft pilot or aviator is a person who controls the flight of an aircraft
by operating its directional flight controls. Some other aircrew members,
such as navigators or flight engineers, are also considered aviators,
because they are involved in
operating the aircraft's navigation and engine systems.

Other aircrew members, such as
drone operators, flight attendants, mechanics and ground crew, are not classified as aviators.
